Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

At AG Wallpaper, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This policy explains how we handle data within our mobile application (package: aneh.handsome.ag5).

Advanced System Integration

To provide advanced customization features, AG Wallpaper utilizes system-level interfaces. We prioritize local processing, meaning your data stays on your device.

Storage Access Framework (SAF) We use Android’s Storage Access Framework to allow you to select specific directories for wallpaper storage. We only access the folders you explicitly grant permission to.
Shizuku & iAdb Integration For advanced features requiring elevated privileges, we support Shizuku and iAdb. These tools function as a bridge; our app does not store or transmit your system credentials or debug tokens to any external server. All commands are executed locally on your device.

Information Collection

AG Wallpaper is designed to function without collecting personally identifiable information. We do not have accounts, and we do not track your identity.

However, we use trusted third-party partners to support app functionality:

  • Google Play Services: For core app functionality.
  • AdMob: To support development through non-personalized advertisements.

Technical Log Data

In the event of a crash or error, technical "Log Data" may be generated. This includes your device name, OS version, and the state of the app at the time of the error. This is used exclusively for debugging and improving the user experience.

Security

While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we follow industry best practices to protect your configuration data. By utilizing Shizuku and SAF, we leverage Android's native security model to ensure your data remains sandboxed.
