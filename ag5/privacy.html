Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 12, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Ryumoto GFX ("Application") for mobile devices. The Application serves as an informational guide, providing item builds, strategies, and performance optimization tips for mobile gaming.

1. Information We Collect and App Permissions

The Application does not require users to register an account or provide personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, or phone number) to use its features.

Advanced System Permissions & API Usage

To provide performance optimization features, the Application utilizes advanced Android APIs and frameworks. All processing done via these mechanisms happens strictly locally on your device:

  • Storage Access Framework (SAF): We use SAF to allow you to grant permissions to specific directories for file management and optimization purposes. The Application only reads or modifies files necessary for its core functionality and does not upload your files to any external servers.
  • Shizuku / IADB (Wireless Debugging): If enabled by the user, the Application interacts with Shizuku or utilizes Android Debug Bridge (IADB) protocols over wireless debugging to execute elevated system commands required for performance optimization. No personal data, device credentials, or system logs are collected or transmitted through these interfaces.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application may collect certain non-personal information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device's unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, and information about the way you use the Application.

2. Third-Party Services & Advertising (Google AdMob)

This Application utilizes third-party advertising services to fund its development. Specifically, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may use tracking technologies to collect anonymized data about your device and ad interactions to serve targeted, relevant advertisements. This data may include your device's Advertising ID (e.g., Android AAID), cookies, and general location data.

For more information on how Google uses your data, please review their policy: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

3. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us access to your device's optimization layers. Because we utilize SAF, Shizuku, and IADB strictly for local operations, your system configurations remain entirely on your device. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us immediately.
